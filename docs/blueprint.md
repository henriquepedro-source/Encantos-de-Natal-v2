# **App Name**: Natal Aconchegante

## Core Features:

- Hero Section: Visually appealing hero section with a headline, subtitle, CTA button, and ebook mockup.
- Connection Section: Section with empathetic text and a cozy Christmas image to establish a personal connection.
- Benefits Section: Highlights the key benefits users will gain, presented with icons and concise text.
- Product Showcase: Detailed section showcasing the ebook with mockups and bullet points of included features.
- Transformation Section: Cards visually illustrating how the product transforms different areas of the home.
- Testimonials Section: Social proof section with reader testimonials and star ratings.
- FAQ Section: Interactive FAQ section using an accordion style for common questions.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Burnt red (#B23B3B) evokes the warmth and traditional feel of Christmas.
- Background color: Off-white (#FFF8F2) creates a clean and cozy backdrop.
- Accent color: Light gold (#E2CBA7) adds a touch of elegance and festive spirit, drawing the user's eyes.
- Headline font: 'Playfair' (serif) for elegant and prominent titles.
- Body font: 'Lato' (sans-serif) ensures clear and modern readability.
- Minimalist icons from Lucide Icons or Phosphor Icons for a clean and modern feel.
- Maximum width of 1200px with ample margins for a spacious and breathable design.
- Smooth scroll behavior, hover animations on buttons, and subtle fade-in animations to enhance user experience.
